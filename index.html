The Student Council

The Student Council of Jain Vasavi Campus which was formed in the year 2016 holds the responsibility of integrating academic and co-curricular spheres of student’s lives. It plays an integral part in curating various intra and inter-collegiate events. The Student Council of Jain college Vasavi campus also strives towards the academic, professional and holistic development of the students individually and as a group, in order to achieve overall excellence.

About JAIN COLLEGE VASAVI CAMPUS

Jain College Vasavi Campus was set up in the year 2016. It is one of the leading colleges in Bangalore imparting quality education at the Undergraduate and Postgraduate levels. The College is well-placed to serve varied needs of students. Learning at Jain College Vasavi Campus is delivered by highly accomplished faculty who possess a wide range of expertise. The learning curriculum is deeply reflective while also being rigorous and relevant to the contemporary world. The commerce and management programs offered at Jain College Vasavi Campus have strong foundations in academic research, and are designed to tackle real issues that are faced in today’s international business community.
